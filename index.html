Multi-Modal AI-powered Search and Rescue Quadcopter

Our quadcopter is designed to enhance search and rescue missions with AI-powered navigation and real-time camera and radar based person detection

Our prototype at Fire and Rescue Station, Kothamangalam.

Applications

Post-Disaster Survivor Detection

The drone's AI-powered vision system and thermal imaging can detect human presence in rubble or flooded regions, transmitting precise survivor locations to rescue teams.

Low-Visibility Search Missions

The drone's infrared and radar-based sensing penetrates through poor visibility conditions, enabling search operations even in complete darkness.

Avalanche Rescue & Snow Patrol

With its terrain-adaptive flight capabilities and radar-assisted scanning, the drone can identify heat signatures beneath snow layers, guiding rescue teams faster than manual searches.

Remote Wilderness & Mountain Rescue

AI-assisted object detection and real-time mapping help locate individuals in vast, rugged terrains, reducing search time and increasing survival rates.

Maritime Search & Coastal Surveillance

The drone's ocean-mapping AI and thermal cameras scan large water areas efficiently, locating survivors and guiding rescue ships toward them.
